### Research Spotlight

* **AIRSEC: Multimodal Keystroke Inference via WiFi and Acoustic Side-Channels**  
  Built a zero-association eavesdropping system that passively extracts CSI through IEEE 802.11 ACK mechanisms and fuses acoustic pulses as timing anchors. The system models inter-keystroke timing and key duration with DTW-based fine-grained features, reducing the search space of 6-digit PINs by over **99%**.
